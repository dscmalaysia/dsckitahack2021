DSCKita Hack 2021

DSCKita Hack 2021 is a 2-day hackathon presented by Developer Student Clubs (DSC) Malaysia



Registration will be closed on 12/03/2021 at 11.59pm

What's KitaHack?

The DSC KitaHack is a 2-day hackathon as a pre-hackathon to prepare students for the 2021 Solution Challenge. Participants will be guided through this hackathon by industry experts to come up with awesome solutions and fine tune them with great impact and scalability! Participants can then take this feedback and experience to refine their projects, preparing for the final submission towards Solution Challenge 2021.

Create your apps with us from scratch. Receive the opportunity to network and learn from our designated speakers and mentors as you refine your solutions to its best form! The announced winner will receive cool swags and prizes from our partners and sponsors. 😎
